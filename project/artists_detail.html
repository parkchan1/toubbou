Ahn Hyunho

Writer Ahn Hyun-ho was born in Yeonje-gu, Busan in 1988 and was born as the eldest son of two sons and one daughter. Since I was young, I have been interested in architecture and art, followed my father, who is an architect, around many sites, and grew up feeling the basics of architecture on the spot. After graduating from Busan High School, he entered Busan National University's Department of Architecture to learn architectural art in earnest, and won prizes in various competitions during the semester to gain more full-scale knowledge. It is introduced as a collection of architecture and contemporary art that you want to see forever, combining modern art and architecture with beauty and practicality.

안현호

안현호 작가는 1988년 부산광역시 연재구 태생이며, 2남1녀중 장남으로 태어났다. 어려서부터 건축물과 미술에 관심이 많았으며 건축가인 아버지를 따라 많은 현장을 따라다녀 건축의 기본을 현장에서 보고 느끼며 커왔다. 부산고등학교를 졸업한후 본격적으로 건축미술을 배우고자 부산대학교 건축학과를 진학을 했으며 학기중 여러 대회를 나가 입상을 하여 좀더 전묵적인 지식을 얻게되었다고 한다. 그렇게하여 현대미술과 건축물을 합하여 아름답과 실용적이며, 두고두고 보고싶은 건축과 현대미술의 집합체로 소개되고 있다.
